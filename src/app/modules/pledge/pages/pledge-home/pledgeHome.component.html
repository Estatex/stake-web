Frequently asked questions

  • What is a SAFT agreement?

    A SAFT agreement is an agreement that states the purpose of your funds and the equivalent in tokens in a legally binding document. In short this means, what we say what we will do with your funds, we will adhere to.

  • If i deposit 1% of the total pool, what happens?

    This means that 1% the total amount of tokens acquired at list will be send straight to a wallet! No vesting will apply since the tokens have been bought directly on an exchange.

  • Am I able to pledge 2 or possibly 3 times in this pool?

    Yes! You can pledge an many times as you want!

  • Is there a maximum one can pledge?

    No, there is no maximum one can pledge. You are completely free to decide how much you want to pledge.
